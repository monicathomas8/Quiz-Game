Rules of the game

Race against the timer to answer questions about your favourate friends TV show.

  1. Answer questions before the timer runs out.
  2. There is a total of 10 levels.
  3. Each level have 1 question.
  4. You must answer the question correctly to move onto the next level.
  5. If you give an incorrect answer, you move down a level.
  6. Answer as many questions as you can before the timer runs out.
  7. If you beat the timer, your usermane and time will be added to the Hall of Fame.
